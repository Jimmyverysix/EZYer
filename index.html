An interactive simulation of high school environment powered by generative AI agents, demonstrating realistic social dynamics and educational interactions.

Abstract

We present EZYer, a novel simulation framework that recreates high school environments using generative AI agents. Our system models complex social interactions between students, teachers, and administrators through large language models. The simulation demonstrates emergent behaviors including friendship formation, peer pressure dynamics, and classroom participation patterns. By grounding the agents in a persistent virtual environment with memory and reflection capabilities, we observe realistic longitudinal development of social relationships. The platform provides a testbed for educational researchers to study adolescent development and for AI researchers to advance agent architectures.
